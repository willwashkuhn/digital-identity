ABOUT ME

Who I am and why I code

Creative, energetic, and capable. That's how I've come to describe myself. I code because the next challenge is always right around the corner. Once I wrap my head around a problem, I will find a solution. My work becomes that much more exciting when I get to learn and adapt a new technology.

What I do and my experience

When solving a problem, I find it's best to break it down into parts and tackle them one at a time. Nothing can stop me when I've got a little momentum going. I've spent the last few years in the sports industry, where projects and requirements can change after every whistle and the thirst for new content is at an all-time high. The products that I supported spanned several languages, dozens of sports, and thousands of leagues around the globe. I know what it's like to get the midnight call, manage a live issue, and grind out a win.

My goals

At this stage of my career and my life, the emphasis is experience. More tech. More meaningful interactions. More successes, and even more failures. I want to continue working on things that I'm passionate about. I also want to start using my voice more by bringing my background of organization, leadership, and recruitment to whatever I'm doing. Perhaps most of all, I want to surround myself with the people that can help me succeed.
